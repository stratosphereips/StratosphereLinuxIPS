Video and equations

The systemic immune-control layer was adapted from the Fields Institute talk Modelling Immunity by Jane Heffernan and the linked paper model.

video fields page paper 
dV/dt = input - α16 V A - γv V
dT/dt = μ21 V - γt T
dF/dt = μ32 T - γf F - α37 F C
dI/dt = μ42 T - γi I - α45 I B
dB/dt = μ52 T + α54 (I / (Si + I)) B - γb B
dA/dt = μ65 B - γa A - α61 A V
dC/dt = μ71 V + α73 (F / (Sf + F)) C - γc C

Here the simulator uses V as effective systemic antigen derived from the 2D pathogen field, then couples F, I, A, B, and C back into the spatial tissue dynamics.
